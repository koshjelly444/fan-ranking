Glossary

Score
Composite engagement score (0–100) calculated from spending, purchase frequency, variety, tenure, touchpoints, subscription tier, and livestream attendance.
Superfan
Top 5% of all paying fans by score. The most engaged and highest-spending supporters.
Core Supporter
Top 20% of paying fans by score. Consistent buyers with strong engagement.
Engaged
All other paying fans who have made at least one purchase but fall outside the top 20%.
Active Follower
Non-buyers who have 2+ touchpoints (email, IG, phone) or have been fans for 1+ year.
Passive
Fans with minimal engagement — fewer than 2 touchpoints, no purchases, and less than 1 year tenure.
Touchpoints
Number of contact channels on file for a fan: email address, Instagram handle, and/or phone number (0–3).
Tenure
How long someone has been a fan, measured from their join date to today.
Twin / Kinfolk / Bestie
Paid subscription tiers on the platform. "Twin" is the highest tier. Having any tier grants a score bonus.
Purchase Variety
Number of distinct purchase categories (e.g., merch, tickets, albums, tracks, livestreams, bundles). More variety = higher score.
Spending (log scale)
Dollar amount spent, scored on a logarithmic scale so the difference between $0→$50 matters more than $500→$550. Saturates around $500+.
Livestream Attendance
Whether the fan has purchased a livestream or listening party ticket.
